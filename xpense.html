Xpense

Xpense app is great help for those who have a limited budget but are bad at keeping it. It can be best for the youngsters, as they do not have any control over how they spend money. We have developed the Xpense app that provides the report of monthly expenses. Whether users want to track personal costs or daily expenses, Xpense app helps to create spending records

We tried to make our application look minimalistic and simple so that it will be easy for users to use the application and to understand the functionality. It is a very easy process from start to end by which user can track their expenses. Firstly, new users must register themselves from signup page and login to their account by providing their email and password. after this they must input their monthly income and save it. Then user will be able to add their expenses one by one after saving their monthly income. From the add expenses page user can check the list of total expenses he/she added till now. From this page user can add more expenses or also he/she can view their report from which they can see how much expenses they have on monthly bases. Report also shows the monthly savings according to the income and expenses
