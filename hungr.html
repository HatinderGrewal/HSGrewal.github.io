Hungr

Hungr is application specially designed for people who do not want to cook their food daily at home because of their busy schedules. Many people eat restaurant food daily which is not healthy for human body. these kind of foods always contins lot of fats. Therefore, this application is to help these kind of people. Also, student are the one of the major target audience of Hungr because students have to do lots of college work and assignments. due to which sometime students don't have much time to prepare their food daily at home.

By this application user can subscribe to any available service in their area or locality. This subscription include two meals per day for one month. These meals contain home made food which is healthy for human body. User can login to their account or creat a new account in this application. Also, indivduals can cancel their subscription any time.
